Hello! My name’s Connor Young and I’m a third year student in Concordia University’s Computer Games program. I have been programming since my time at Dawson College’s computer science program and after completing the highly practical three year program I wanted a deeper understanding of the theory behind computer science and to start specializing. Video games have always been a big passion of mine and I find myself drawn to all the skills needed to make a game. I enjoy drawing and playing music, but to me programming has been the biggest attraction because it’s the glue which makes a game world interactable. Of course, I also enjoy the challenge of programming! There’s always many different ways to accomplish your task and there are some ways more elegant than others. I find fine-tuning a program to run efficiently and simply to be fun and in computer games I find more and more opportunities to do what I enjoy.
